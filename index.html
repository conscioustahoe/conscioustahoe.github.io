Mental Health

Introducing therapy.me

therapy.me lets people do personalized cognitive behavioural therapy without a therapist. CBT is a science-backed, goal-oriented, structured and highly effective treatment for anxiety and depression, but most people can't afford a therapist to administer it. therapy.me will assess a users needs, teach them relevant CBT concepts, walk them through interactive exercises, keep them motivated, and measure their progress.
