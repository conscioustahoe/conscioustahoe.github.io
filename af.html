Introducing artificial friend

AF (Artificial Friend) is an iOS app that lets anyone create, share and use AI-powered mini apps. Users can create a mini app using prompts and no-code building blocks like voice recorder, flashcards, progress tracker, generative text and images. A mini-app can be be anything you can make from the available building blocks for eg. a programming language tutor that can create a syllabus for you, quiz you and track your progress.
