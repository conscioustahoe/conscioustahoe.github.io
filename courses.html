Principles of fMRI

Offered By: Johns Hopkins University

Course Duration: Approx. 2 weeks

In this course I learned about design, acquisition and analysis of fMRI data including psychological and behavioral inferences, preprocessing of fMRI data, Generalized Linear Models, brain connectivity and multi-voxel pattern analysis.

Practical deep learning for coders

Offered By: fast.ai

Course Duration: Approx. 3 months

In this course I learned how to build and train deep learning models for computer vision, natural language processing, tabular analysis, and collaborative filtering problems. I also learned how to turn your models into web applications, and deploy them on Hugging Face Spaces, why and how deep learning models work, and how to use that knowledge to improve the accuracy, speed, and reliability of your models and the latest deep learning techniques that really matter in practice.
